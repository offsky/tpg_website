RULES

How to Participate

Everyone is welcome to come watch. You will need a current AMA membership to participate. A TPG membership gets you a discount on the entry fee. Don’t be discouraged if you are not flying the latest design. As long as it is safe to fly, come out and have fun!

Entry is $5 per class for TPG members. For non-members it is $10 for the first class and $5 for each additional class.

Event registration and payment will be through F3X Vault, with pilots self-entering scores on their phones. Entry will generally close one day before the contest. Registration details for each event will be provided in advance on our Discord server and Google Group.

Contest Location

Contests will be held at the Poway Field. Our ongoing use of these properties is totally dependent on being good guests. Please respect the rules and make safe flying your number one priority.

For 2022, TPG is continuing with TD and F5J formats, alternating each month. This is a 10-
contest series, with no events in August or December. Unless otherwise noted, TD events will
be held at the Encinitas Flight Center and F5J events at the Poway Flight Center.

Who can participate?

All pilots are welcome. You just need current AMA membership, and being a TPG member gets
you a discount on the entry fee. Don’t be discouraged if you are not flying the latest high-dollar
molded ship. As long as it is safe to fly, come out and have fun!

Contest Formats

TD events – there are two competition classes: Unlimited and RES. Unlimited may be flown with
any non-motorized sailplane. RES may be flown by any non-motorized sailplane controlled only
by rudder, elevator and spoiler (no movable trailing edge may be used on the primary wing).

F5J events – these will be conducted according to the U.S. F5J Tour rules (with no flyoffs).
Current F5J Tour rules: http://www.f5j-usa.com/contest-rules/

Current FAI F5J rules: http://www.f5j-usa.com/f5j-rules/

All contests will have at least four rounds, subject to weather or other conditions. Contest
format and tasks will be announced on the TPG Google Group and RC Groups in advance.

Entry Fees

TD events – $5 per class for TPG members; $10 for non-members (one class), $15 for two
classes.

F5J events – $5 for TPG members; $10 for non-members

Contest Locations

TD contests will be flown at the Encinitas Flight Center. Field use restriction: if there is at least
0.25 inches of rain within the five days immediately preceding a contest, the contest will either
be rescheduled or cancelled. No motorized planes are allowed at the Encinitas field.

F5J contests will be flown at the Poway Flight Center.

Event registration and payment will be through F3X Vault, with pilots self-entering scores on
their phones/mobile devices. F5J contest entries may be capped for logistics reasons, and entry
will generally close one day before the contest. Registration details for each event will be
provided in advance.

Contests generally start at 9:00 with a pilot’s meeting shortly before.
