Poway Field

The Poway Field is a private field that is the home to our club's F3K / DLG Contests, our F5J Contests, our Foamy Fun Fly events as well as general TD fun flying in North County.

This field is first and foremost a glider field for TPG members only. Only sailplanes and F5J electric sailplanes are allowed. Sailplanes of all types may be launched by hand, hi-start, or winch. For F5J, only electric motors can be used for launching (30 second maximum motor run) to altitude then soaring as per usual thermal duration practice.

What Is Required To Fly Here?

  • AMA membership. If you are a visitor please be prepared to show your card.
  • Membership in the Torrey Pines Gulls (or be accompanied by a TPG member).
  • Everyone must obey all AMA and local flight regulations.

Restricted Flight Zones

Due to the close proximity of the horse facility there are strict limits on where flight is permitted. If you violate the zones you risk losing your flight privilege at the field and possibly jeopardize the privilege to fly here for everyone. Powered flight

At Poway, we have permission to use limited-run electric motors as a launch option, following the F5J/K format. Motor runs are limited to 30 seconds for launch only and in contests must be governed using a competition recording altimeter switch specifically designed for this purpose. For recreational flying, you must still adhere to the 30 second launch-only motor run limit but can do so by simply timing the run. Re-start in flight is only allowed in the case of imminent risk.
It must be emphasized – even though we allow electric motor-assisted launch at Poway, all other forms of powered flight are not permitted. Accordingly, we have a set of special rules in
place for F5J/K at Poway. Absolutely no electric sport aircraft or drone flying of any kind is allowed at Poway other than F5J electric powered gliders.

Horses and People

The areas surrounding the field are an active horse facility. As such, horses and people are likely to be nearby. Even if you are in the fly-zone you are responsible for maintaining a safe distance between your plane and the horses and other people.

We Are Guests Here

Our ongoing use of this property is totally dependent on being good guests. Please respect the rules and make safe flying your number one priority. Pick up your trash, lock the gate when you leave, and maintain a peaceful and quiet flying area.
